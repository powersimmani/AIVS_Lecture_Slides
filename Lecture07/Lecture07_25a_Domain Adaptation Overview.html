Popular Methods
DANN (Domain-Adversarial NN)
Gradient reversal layer makes features domain-invariant while maintaining task performance.
CORAL (Correlation Alignment)
Align second-order statistics (covariance) of source and target features.
MMD (Maximum Mean Discrepancy)
Kernel-based distance metric between distributions in RKHS.
