Inpainting & Outpainting
Filling missing regions and extending images with diffusion models
Inpainting
How It Works
1. Mask region to fill
2. During diffusion: keep known pixels fixed
3. Only denoise masked region
4. Result: Coherent fill matching context
Techniques
RePaint: Resample at each step
Blended Diffusion: Gradual blending
Fine-tuned: Train with mask conditioning
Applications
- Object removal
- Image restoration
- Content replacement
Outpainting
How It Works
1. Place original image in larger canvas
2. Treat outer region as "masked"
3. Generate content extending from edges
4. Seamlessly expands the scene
Challenges
- Maintaining global coherence
- Consistent lighting/perspective
- Avoiding repetitive patterns
- Edge artifacts
Applications
- Extending photos (landscapes, rooms)
- Creating panoramas
- Aspect ratio conversion
- "Uncropping" images
