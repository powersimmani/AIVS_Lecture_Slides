Consistency Models
One-step generation from diffusion models (Song et al., 2023)
Core Idea
The Problem with Diffusion
Diffusion models need 20-1000 denoising steps.
Each step = one neural network forward pass.
Slow inference!
Consistency Model Insight
Learn to map any point on the trajectory directly to the clean image.

f(x_t, t) = x_0 for all t

One step from any noise level to clean!

Training Methods
1. Consistency Distillation:
Distill from pre-trained diffusion model

2. Consistency Training:
Train from scratch without teacher model

3. LCM (Latent Consistency):
Apply to latent diffusion (Stable Diffusion)
Practical Impact
Stable Diffusion: 50 steps, ~5 seconds
With LCM: 4 steps, ~0.5 seconds
10x faster with similar quality!
