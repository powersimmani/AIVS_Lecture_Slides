Modern Activation Functions: GELU & Swish
Standard choices for Transformers and efficient architectures
GELU Transformer Standard
GELU(x) = x · Φ(x)
≈ 0.5x(1 + tanh[√(2/π)(x + 0.044715x³)])
✓Φ(x) = CDF of standard normal
✓Smooth approximation to ReLU
✓Non-monotonic (unlike ReLU)
✓Stochastic regularization effect
✓Hendrycks & Gimpel (2016)
Used in: BERT, GPT, ViT, RoBERTa, T5
Swish / SiLU Auto-discovered
Swish(x) = x · σ(βx)
SiLU(x) = x · σ(x) when β=1
✓Google Brain automated search (2017)
✓Self-gated (x gates itself)
✓β can be learnable parameter
✓Better gradient flow in deep nets
✓Ramachandran et al. (2017)
Used in: EfficientNet, MobileNetV3, Mish variant
