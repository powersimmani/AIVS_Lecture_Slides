Temporal Convolutional Networks (TCN)
CNN-based alternative to RNN for sequence modeling
Core Concepts
Key Idea: Use 1D convolutions with two special properties:
1. Causal: Output at time t depends only on inputs at time ≤ t
2. Dilated: Exponentially increasing receptive field
Causal Convolution
No future leakage
