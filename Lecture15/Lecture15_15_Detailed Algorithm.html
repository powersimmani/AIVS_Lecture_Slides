Part 3/6: Training Algorithm
Detailed Algorithm
Training Procedure
1 Sample minibatch of m noise samples {z(1)...z(m)}
2 Sample minibatch of m real samples {x(1)...x(m)}
3 Update Discriminator by ascending gradient:
∇θd [1/m Σ (log D(x(i)) + log(1-D(G(z(i)))))]
Switch to Generator
4 Sample new minibatch of noise samples
5 Update Generator by descending gradient:
∇θg [1/m Σ log(1-D(G(z(i))))]
6 Repeat until convergence
Update Strategy
D Ascending gradient (maximize)
G Descending gradient (minimize)
Notation
m Minibatch size
z Noise vector
x Real sample
θd D parameters
θg G parameters
⟲
Iterate Until Equilibrium
