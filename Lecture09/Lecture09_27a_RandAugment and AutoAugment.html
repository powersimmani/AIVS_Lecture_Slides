Automated Data Augmentation
Learning optimal augmentation policies automatically
AutoAugment
Google, 2018
Idea: Use reinforcement learning to search for optimal augmentation policy on target dataset.
Search Space
16 operations, 10 magnitudes, 11 probabilities
✓Best accuracy (dataset-specific)
•15,000 GPU hours search
•Reuse policies possible
•ImageNet policy transferable
RandAugment
Google, 2020
Idea: Random selection is as good as learned policy! Just tune N (ops) and M (magnitude).
Hyperparameters
N = 2, M = 9 (common default)
✓No search required
✓2 hyperparams only
✓AutoAugment-level accuracy
✓Works across datasets
TrivialAugment
2021
Idea: Even simpler - apply one random operation with random magnitude per image.
Hyperparameters
None! (or just M)
✓Simplest approach
✓Zero tuning needed
✓SOTA performance
✓Most robust
