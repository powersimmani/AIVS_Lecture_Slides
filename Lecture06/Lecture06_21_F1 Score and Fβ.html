F1 Score and Fβ
F1 Score
Harmonic Mean
F1 = 2 × P × R / (P + R)
Balances Precision & Recall
• Single metric (0 to 1)
• Equal weight to P and R
• Penalizes extreme values
• Use when balance needed
Fβ Score
Weighted F-Score
Fβ = (1+β²) × P × R / (β²P + R)
Allows emphasis control
• β controls weight
• β > 1: Favor recall
• β < 1: Favor precision
• β = 1: Standard F1
