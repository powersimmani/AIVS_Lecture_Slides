Pre-LayerNorm vs Post-LayerNorm
Where to place normalization in Transformer blocks
Post-LayerNorm (Original)
h = LayerNorm(x + Attention(x))
out = LayerNorm(h + FFN(h))
Characteristics
- Original Transformer (Vaswani 2017)
- Needs learning rate warmup
- Training can be unstable for deep models
- Better final performance (sometimes)
Pre-LayerNorm (Modern)
h = x + Attention(LayerNorm(x))
out = h + FFN(LayerNorm(h))
Characteristics
- GPT-2, GPT-3, LLaMA, etc.
- No warmup needed (stable from start)
- Easier to train very deep models
- Direct gradient path through residual
