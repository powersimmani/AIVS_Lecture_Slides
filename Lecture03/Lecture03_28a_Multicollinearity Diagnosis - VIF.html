Multicollinearity Diagnosis: VIF
Variance Inflation Factor - Detecting correlated predictors
‚ö†Ô∏è Multicollinearity Problems
Coefficient estimates become unstable
Standard errors inflate (wide CIs)
Signs may flip unexpectedly
Individual p-values misleading
(XTX)-1 nearly singular
üìä VIF Formula
VIFj = 1 / (1 - R2j)
Where R¬≤j = R-squared from regressing Xj on all other predictors
Higher VIF = More collinearity with other features
üìè VIF Interpretation Guide
VIF Value Interpretation Action
1 No correlation None needed
1 - 5 Moderate Usually OK
5 - 10 High Caution needed
> 10 Severe Action required
‚úì Solutions
1. Remove variables: Drop one of correlated pair
2. Combine variables: Create composite score
3. PCA: Use principal components instead
4. Ridge regression: L2 penalty handles it
5. Collect more data: If feasible
