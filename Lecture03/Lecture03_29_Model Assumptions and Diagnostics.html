Model Assumptions and Diagnostics
Key Assumptions
1. Linearity
True relationship is linear (or approximately)
2. Independence
Observations are independent
3. Homoscedasticity
Constant error variance across X
4. Normality
Errors ~ N(0, σ²)
5. No Multicollinearity
Predictors not highly correlated
