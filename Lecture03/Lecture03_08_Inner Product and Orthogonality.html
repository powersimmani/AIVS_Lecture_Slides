Inner Product and Orthogonality
Inner Product (Dot Product)
⟨x, y⟩ = x₁y₁ + x₂y₂ + ... + xₙyₙ
Geometric Interpretation
⟨x, y⟩ = ‖x‖‖y‖cos(θ)
Measures alignment between vectors
Norm (Length)
‖x‖ = √(⟨x, x⟩) = √(x₁² + x₂² + ... + xₙ²)
Orthogonality
x ⊥ y when ⟨x, y⟩ = 0
Vectors are perpendicular (θ = 90°)
Orthonormal Basis
Basis vectors with unit length, mutually orthogonal
Projection
