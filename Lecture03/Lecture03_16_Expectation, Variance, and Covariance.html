Expectation, Variance, and Covariance
Expectation E[X]
Average value
E[X] = Σ xP(X=x) or ∫ xf(x)dx
Properties
E[aX + b] = aE[X] + b
E[X + Y] = E[X] + E[Y]
Variance Var(X)
Spread around mean
Var(X) = E[(X - μ)²]
Standard Deviation σ
σ = √Var(X)
Covariance
Cov(X,Y) = E[(X - μₓ)(Y - μᵧ)]
Positive: X and Y increase together
