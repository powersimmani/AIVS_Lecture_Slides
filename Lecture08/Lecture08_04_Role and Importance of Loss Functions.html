Loss Function
Role & Importance
1. Quantification Quantifies difference between predicted and actual values
2. Gradient Direction Guides optimization process by providing gradient direction
3. Task-Specific Design Different loss functions needed per task (regression vs classification)
4. Performance Impact Direct impact on model performance and behavior
5. Objective Function Acts as the objective function that the model seeks to minimize
6. Convergence & Accuracy Affects convergence speed and final model accuracy
