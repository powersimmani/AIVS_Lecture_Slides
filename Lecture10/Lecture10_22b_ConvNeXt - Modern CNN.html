ConvNeXt: A ConvNet for the 2020s
Modernizing ResNet with Transformer design choices (Meta, 2022)
Key Modifications: ResNet → ConvNeXt
1
Macro Design
Stage ratio (3,4,6,3) → (3,3,9,3)
Stem: 7×7 conv → 4×4 conv, stride 4 (patchify)
2
ResNeXt-ify
Use depthwise separable convolution
Width: 64 → 96 channels
3
Inverted Bottleneck
Expand → Depthwise → Compress
Like MobileNet v2 / Transformer FFN
4
Large Kernel
3×3 → 7×7 depthwise conv
Mimics ViT's large receptive field
5
Micro Design
GELU activation, LayerNorm (not BN)
Fewer normalization layers
