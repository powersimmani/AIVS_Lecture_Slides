📦 Padding Strategy
Add special PAD tokens to shorter sequences to match the longest sequence in batch.
pad_sequence(sequences,
  batch_first=True,
  padding_value=0)
🎭 Use Masking
Create masks to ignore PAD tokens during attention computation and loss calculation.
mask = (input != PAD)
scores.masked_fill(
  ~mask, -inf)
